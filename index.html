About International Yoga Day

International Yoga Day is celebrated every year on 21st June across the world to promote the importance of yoga for physical fitness, mental peace, and overall well-being. Yoga is an ancient practice that helps improve flexibility, concentration, breathing control, and stress management.

The Government of India and various international organizations actively promote yoga awareness programs to encourage healthier lifestyles. This participation program is designed to inspire individuals to adopt yoga as part of their daily routine.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the International Yoga Day Participation Program?

It's an online awareness initiative designed to promote yoga and healthy living. Participants receive a digital participation certificate after registration.

Who can participate?

Anyone can participate—students, teachers, professionals, homemakers, senior citizens, and general public. No age limit or prior experience required.

Is prior yoga experience required?

No, prior yoga experience is not mandatory. The program is open to everyone regardless of experience level.

How do I register?

Fill out the online registration form on this page with your personal details and submit it. Your certificate will be generated instantly.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all eligible participants receive a digital participation certificate that can be viewed, downloaded, and verified online.

What details are required for registration?

You need to provide: Full Name, Email, Mobile Number, Complete Address, and optionally your Study Center/Institute name.

Is the certificate free?

Yes! Registration and certificate issuance are completely free for all participants.

Can I participate from any state in India?

Yes, participants from all states and union territories of India can join the program.

Can institutions participate?

Yes, schools, colleges, coaching centers, NGOs, and training institutes can encourage their students and members to participate.

How is the certificate verified?

Each certificate has a unique certificate number that can be verified using the official verification system on this website.
